Support

If you need help setting up meteo, consider asking the mailing list. You must be subscribed to the mailing list if you want to post, send a message with the single word subscribe in the body to meteo-request@lists.othello.ch.

You may also send mail to the author, but your problem may actually take longer to be resolved, as I cannot always respond in a timely manner.
